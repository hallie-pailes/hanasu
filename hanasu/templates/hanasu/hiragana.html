What is Hiragana?

  • In Japanese, there are 3 kinds of characters: Hiragana, Katakana, and Kanji. Both Hiragana and Katakana represent different sounds, but are used differently.
  • Hiragana is used for conjugation endings, function words, and words that are native to Japanese.
