Hanasu - A Japanese learning and study resource

Welcome to Hanasu, a Japanese study resource! Here you can learn and study Hiragana, Katakana, and go over lessons and do activities to help you learn Japanese. The lessons and activities will be based around the style and pacing of the Genki: An Integrated Course in Elementary Japanese textbooks (2nd Edition).

There are lesson sections that contain quizzes over the material for that particular lesson, along with hiragana and katakana sections that have both charts and quizzes for each.

Hiragana Chart
- Chart containing all hiragana and their sounds
Hiragana Quiz
- Quiz over all hiragana
- You will be given the hiragana and need to input it's correct sound!

Katakana Chart
- Chart containing all katakana and their sounds
Katakana Quiz
- Quiz over all katakana
- You will be given the katakana and need to input it's correct sound!
